Turn your iPhone into a professional sound level meter. Measures ambient noise in real-time decibels (dB) using the built-in microphone.

FEATURES
- Real-time dB measurement at 10 readings per second
- Large analog-style needle gauge with smooth animation
- Digital readout: current dB plus min, max, average and peak
- Peak hold indicator with one-tap reset
- Noise exposure time calculator based on NIOSH reference levels
- Color-coded levels: green (safe), orange (moderate), red (dangerous)
- Scrolling session chart of sound level over time
- Calibration offset to match a known reference
- Pause and resume measurement at any time
- Full dark mode and VoiceOver accessibility
- Localized in English, Japanese and German

PERFECT FOR
Musicians checking venue levels, workers assessing workplace noise, parents monitoring nursery sound, or anyone curious about their environment.

PRIVACY FIRST
The microphone is used only to measure sound levels. No audio is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted. No internet connection is required and no data is collected.

PLEASE NOTE
iPhone microphones are not laboratory calibrated, so readings are an approximation and should not be used for legal, medical, or occupational compliance.
