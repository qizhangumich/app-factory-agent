Privacy Policy

Sound Level Meter · Last updated: 22 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sound Level Meter app ("the App") handles information. We have designed the App to work entirely on your device and to collect no personal data whatsoever.

Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone for one purpose only: to measure the sound pressure level (loudness, in decibels) of your surroundings in real time.

  • No audio is recorded. The App reads only the audio power level reported by the system metering interface.
  • No audio is stored. Nothing you say or any sound around you is saved to disk in any usable form.
  • No audio is transmitted. The App has no networking code and never sends audio or any other content off your device.

Data We Collect

None. The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. It contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

The only information the App stores is your calibration offset preference, saved locally on your device using the system preferences store (UserDefaults). This value never leaves your device and can be reset within the App at any time. Session statistics and the history chart exist only in memory while the App is open and are discarded when you close it.

Internet and Third Parties

The App functions fully offline. It does not require an internet connection, does not use any third-party services, and does not integrate any external data processors.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Accuracy Disclaimer

iPhone microphones are not laboratory calibrated. Readings provided by the App are approximations and must not be relied upon for legal, medical, or occupational-safety compliance purposes.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with the App and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the App Store listing for Sound Level Meter.
