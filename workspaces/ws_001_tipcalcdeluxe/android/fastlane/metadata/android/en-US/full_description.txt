A simple, clean way to split any bill. Enter the total, set your tip percentage, add tax if needed, and instantly see what each person owes. Supports unequal splits for when someone ordered the lobster. Clean, ad-free, works offline. One tap, done.

FEATURES
- Custom tip percentage with a smooth 0-50% slider and quick-pick presets
- Optional tax with a configurable rate
- Split between 1 and 20 people
- Unequal splits: assign a custom share to each person
- Clear per-person breakdown of tip, tax and total
- Round the grand total up or down to the nearest dollar
- History of your last 20 calculations, tap to restore
- Currency symbol detected automatically from your region
- Full dark mode support
- Haptic feedback for a tactile, responsive feel

No ads. No subscriptions. No account. No data collection. Just clean, accurate math whenever the check arrives.
